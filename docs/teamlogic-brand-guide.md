Overview

The TeamLogic IT brand encompasses what current and potential customers encounter anytime they see or experience our
name, logo or service. Whether that’s through our products, services, marketing, a sales call, technicians, or even the way in
which the phone is answered, they form an opinion about our company. When they encounter consistency in all we do, it
sends a clear message of strength, cohesion and quality, which are important elements to building a positive brand image.

The purpose of this TeamLogic IT Brand Standards Guide is to help you achieve consistency in the use of the TeamLogic IT
logo as well as other design and messaging vehicles. Be sure to keep this guide as a reference and adhere to its guidelines
at all times. Current versions of this guide, as well as all versions of the TeamLogic IT logo, can be found on the BMSS Library
under Marketing.

BRAND NAME/TRADEMARK

The TeamLogic IT logo is a key vehicle for establishing a strong brand identity. It is a registered brand trademark of
TeamLogic, Inc. and is also representative of your local company name. Here are a few rules for displaying the
TeamLogic IT name:

In written communication:  TeamLogic IT
The brand name is an adjoined word. Note the capital L in the middle without a space, and with a space before IT.

DOMAIN NAME, URL & EMAIL POLICY

All franchisees must adhere to the TeamLogic IT Domain Name, URL and Email Policy, which can be found in the Appendix
at the end of this guide and in the Operations Manual. All URLs containing any variation of the TeamLogic IT name must be
approved by Corporate.

LOGO USAGE

The TeamLogic IT logo is available in both a horizontal and a vertical orientation. The icon and word elements in both
orientations are to be treated as a single unit—do not separate the elements (see examples following).

Altering the colors of the TeamLogic IT logo is not allowed (see examples following).

The TeamLogic IT logo is also representative of your local company name. Altering the logo in any manner to reflect a local
presence is not allowed (see examples following).

As a rule, the TeamLogic IT logo should always be used with the tagline. However, in some cases spacing, sizing or visibility
issues may prevent using the tagline. Discretion and good judgment should be used to ensure the TeamLogic IT brand is
always represented in a professional-looking manner. You cannot use elements from the logo or tagline independently.

Domain Name, URL and Email Policy

To maintain the integrity and online security of the TeamLogic IT brand and to support your business’s
operations, it is important that we outline the Domain Name, URL and Email policy for TeamLogic IT
franchisees. This policy is written to ensure the consistency of the message in the marketplace for the brand,
protect the brand identity, and offer your solutions for partner relationships and pertinent alerts for outages.

Domain names are used to identify the name of a website or email address. It’s what comes after the “@”
symbol in an email address, or after “www.” in a web address. For example, here are several common domain
names: google.com, youtube.com, and microsoft.com. Domain names are used in URLs to identify Web pages.

A URL (Universal Resource Locator) on the other hand is a complete web address used to find a web page.
While the domain is the name of the website, a URL will lead to any one of the pages within the website.
Every URL contains a domain name, as well as other components needed to locate the specific page or piece
of content.

For example, in the URL https://www.teamlogicit.com/torranceca524/, the domain name is teamlogicit.com,
whereas https://www.teamlogicit.com/torranceca524/ represents the complete URL.

URL POLICY

A franchisee may only use the URL owned by TeamLogic, Inc. and assigned to them by TeamLogic, Inc. or
another URL pre-approved by TeamLogic, Inc. that is representative of and exclusive to their territory.

Example URLs:
•  https://www.teamlogicit.com/torranceca524/
•  http://www.teamlogicit.com/TorranceCA

attached Domain Name, URL & Email Request Form (Appendix A) to submit a request to the Franchise
